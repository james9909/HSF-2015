In order to solve the problem, you need to find the hash collision, or the hash that occurs more
than once. We can get the md5 hashes of all the pictures by running:

    $ md5sum * > hashes.txt

Then, we can find the hash that occurs multiple times via:

    $ sort hashes.txt | uniq -d

253dd04e87492e4fc3471de5e776bc3d
